Tenant Obligations & Structure

  • Direct Borrower Status Financing is established in the tenant's name (via Wisetack, GreenSky, or ELMERS) to ensure clear upfront terms and mitigate landlord flight risk.
  • 24-Month Commitment Mandatory lease duration required to match loan maturity. All financing must be structured for complete payoff by the end of the term.
  • Default Risk Shift If the lease is broken, the debt follows the tenant directly via collections, bypassing the lengthy eviction recovery process.

Tenant Protections & Rights

  • Contractor Accountability Contractors are paid upfront via the sponsor loan. If work is substandard, the tenant has the direct authority to report them to the financing company.
  • Full Account Visibility As the primary account holder, the tenant receives direct statements, ensuring 100% transparency that the landlord is making the agreed-upon payments.
  • Financial Override Power If the landlord fails to make a $450 payment, the tenant has explicit permission to pay the loan directly and deduct the amount from the $1,400 rent.
